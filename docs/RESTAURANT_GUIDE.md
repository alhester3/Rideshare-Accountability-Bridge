# Restaurant Partner Guide

## Restaurant Experience
Restaurant partners see order/priority context, deprioritization alerts, and a request-review path for disputed outcomes.

## Restaurant Actions
- Review order decision signals
- Submit review requests/appeals
- Track appeal outcomes

## Frontend Areas
- `frontend/src/pages/DashboardPage.jsx` (Restaurant dashboard)
- `frontend/src/pages/AppealsPage.jsx` (Restaurant appeal submissions)

## Backend Controllers
- `backend/Controllers/Restaurant/RestaurantOrdersController.cs`
- `backend/Controllers/Restaurant/RestaurantAppealsController.cs`

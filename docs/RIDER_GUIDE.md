# Rider Guide

## What Riders See
Riders see recent ride history, decision explanations, trust indicators, surge alerts, and one-click recourse options.

## Rider Actions
- Open decision explanations
- Request clarification
- Submit appeals
- Track appeal status through review and resolution

## Frontend Areas
- `frontend/src/pages/DashboardPage.jsx` (Rider dashboard)
- `frontend/src/pages/ExplanationPage.jsx` (Decision detail)
- `frontend/src/pages/AppealsPage.jsx` (Appeal submission and tracker)
